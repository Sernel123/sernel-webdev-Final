Travel Dreamer

      Travel Dreamer is a blog about the travels experienced by the author and the dream destinations as well. It contains images from different destinations that has been travelled, the different dream destinations that the author want to travel soon. And also some travel quotes that may inspire you to travel.

      This blog was created because the author wants to share to the people that travelling is a very nice experience. Eventhough he just still travelled the near places but indeed it is a very great experience and the author believed that in the future he can travel the places that he dreamed to travel.

     Dreaming to travel is one of the mostly dreamed of by people especially the young ones nowadays. To travel to different beautiful and wonderful places around the world. And I am one of these people who are dreaming to travel. Travelling to different places is indeed a very nice experiences. It makes us very excited when we will go to a travel. Indeed travelling makes you forget the problems that you are facing. It helps remove stress.Everyone has a dream place to visit once in life, and many get the chance to fulfill their dream trip. In this regard, I also have a dream place to visit with the intention to feel about the people, place, and things of that particular place.
